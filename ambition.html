Ambition

Ambition in relation to investment for impact can mean clear targets and timelines, such as conserving 30% of land-based and marine ecosystems by 2030 or achieving net zero emissions by mid-century.

Ambition can be expressed in terms of specific improved outcomes for people and communities; e.g., sustainably produced food is available to all people, regardless of income. For such an impact goal to be more useful for an investor, the investor will require relevant policy support from agencies at all levels, and the impact will have to be sustained over time. It cannot be measured simply at one moment in time.
